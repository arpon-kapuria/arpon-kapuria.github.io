**G**RE is widely considered as one of the toughest exams in the world, especially for those seeking admission to graduate programs in the United States and Canada. Before starting your GRE prep, make sure the universities you're applying to require it. I took the GRE in June 2024 and scored 313 (Q163, V150). I could have done better but made some mistakes during the Quant section. Nevertheless, here’s my study plan, along with some tips if you’re aiming for a higher score. Let's get started!

> This note is best viewed on a tablet or desktop. Also `V` refers to Verbal & `Q` refers to Quant.

## Overview

This plan follows Gregmat for Verbal only, KMF for both Quant and Verbal, and ETS materials. It's a three-month plan, and everything is written rigorously and in-depth. Follow this plan only if you're doing nothing except taking prep for GRE, as it's a very intensive plan. Don't forget to keep track of your progress every day. 

**Target Score** (Minimum) : `320` with AWA `4` | Quant Score `165` | Verbal Score `155` 

> When using KMF website, translate the website content to english as it's a chinese website. The books mentioned in this blog are available in the Google Drive links shared in the Resources section at the end.

## First Month

**Focus** : Strategy (V & Q) + Big Book (V) + Vocab (V + AWA) + KMF (Q) + ETS V & Q book 

### Verbal

1. Vocabulary : Gregmat's 900 words (Included in 1 month [Gregmat plan](https://www.gregmat.com/study-plans/one-month-study-plan-version-2)) 
2. Follow 1-month [Gregmat plan](https://www.gregmat.com/study-plans/one-month-study-plan-version-2) for verbal only. 
3. Finish BIG BOOK - 27 Tests in 27 days. You can find the [Solution explanation](https://www.gregmat.com/course/old-gre-walkthroughs) here, if you have Gregmat susbcription.
4. GRE Issue Writing - Follow Gregmat's [Dedicated Issue Writing Series](https://www.gregmat.com/course/dedicated-writing) (6 videos only). 
5. Refer to this [Reading comprehension playlist on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLWJh2RiFxeYHC-ZGQhhtzHaOkREV-TA7H) by GRE Instructor Bruce Hanson. This is an underrated GEM. 
6. ETS Verbal Practice Book - Go through everything except Mixed Practice Sets.

### Quant

1. For basics / strategies, you can refer to [The Tested Tutor](https://www.youtube.com/@TheTestedTutor) videos on YouTube.
2. Refer to this book [Nova GRE Math Bible](https://greworldknowlegde.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/nova-gre-math-bible.pdf) for Geometry only. Although some people suggest [Manhattan 5lb](https://www.ircambridge.com/books/manhattan.pdf) and [Manhattan 8 strategy books.](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1NtnGUeWKGdd7r1p1gp3G5BU1EC1t5y_J) But I believe those are just extras. Following the Tested Tutor and Nova GRE Math Bible should be enough.
3. **First 18 days** - solve 170 test points from KMF. 
    - Total 6 sections - 475 questions - 3 days allotted per section
    - KMF Link : [https://gre.kmf.com/practise/qr/238](https://gre.kmf.com/practise/qr/238)
4. **Next 4 days** - solve 170 Hard questions from KMF.
    - Total 17 sections - 4 sections per day (40 Q)
    - KMF Link : [https://gre.kmf.com/practise/qr/237](https://gre.kmf.com/practise/qr/237)
5. **Next 4 days** - Special Graph exercises on KMF.
    - 108 question - 12 sections - 3 sections per day (27 Q)
    - KMF Link : [https://gre.kmf.com/practise/qr/225](https://gre.kmf.com/practise/qr/225)
6. ETS Quant Practice Book - Go through everything except Mixed Practice Sets.

> Write Power Prep 1 and note down the mistakes, where you're lacking.

## Second Month

**Focus** : KMF + Vocab Practice + AWA Practice 

### Verbal

1. **First 16 days –**
    - First 16 days - Sentence Equivalence (Side by side with Single & Double blank). 
        - 480 questions - 48 sections - 3 sections per day (30 Q)
        - KMF Link : [https://gre.kmf.com/practise/tc/220](https://gre.kmf.com/practise/tc/220)
    - First 8 days - Single Blank questions.
        - 240 questions - 24 sections - 3 sections per day (30 Q)
        - KMF Link : [https://gre.kmf.com/practise/tc/223](https://gre.kmf.com/practise/tc/223)
    - Next 8 days - Double Blank questions.
        - 240 questions - 48 sections - 6 sections per day (30 Q)
        - KMF Link : [https://gre.kmf.com/practise/tc/222](https://gre.kmf.com/practise/tc/222)
2. **Next 12 days –**
    - Three blank questions.
        - 180 questions - 36 sections - 3 sections per day (15 Q)
        - KMF Link : [https://gre.kmf.com/practise/tc/221](https://gre.kmf.com/practise/tc/221)
    - Logic questions.
        - 72 questions - 71 sections - 6 sections per day (6 Q)
        - KMF Link : [https://gre.kmf.com/practise/rc/161](https://gre.kmf.com/practise/rc/161)
3. **Across the Month –**
    - 28 days - RC Special Practice Questions. 
        - 270 questions - 133 sections - 5 sections day (10 Q)
        - KMF Link : [https://gre.kmf.com/practise/rc/227](https://gre.kmf.com/practise/rc/227)
    - 28 days - RC Subject specific questions.
        - 102 questions - 17 sections - 1 section per day
        - KMF Link : [https://gre.kmf.com/practise/rc/213](https://gre.kmf.com/practise/rc/213)
    - Practice Issue essay.
        - 1 essay from ETS Issue Pool - per day / every other day 
        - Link : [https://www.ets.org/pdfs/gre/issue-pool.pdf](https://www.ets.org/pdfs/gre/issue-pool.pdf)

### Quant

1. **First 7 days –** Solve special exercises for comparing small and big questions from KMF. 
    - 200 questions - 20 sections - each day 3/4 sections (30 Q)
    - KMF Link : [https://gre.kmf.com/practise/qr/226](https://gre.kmf.com/practise/qr/226)
    - **N.B** : While doing this section - Figure out where you’re lacking and revise
2. **Next 19 days –** Exercise problems with different difficulty levels from KMF.
    - 1000 questions - 74 section - each day 4 sections (12 Q - 15 Q - 12 Q - 15 Q)
    - KMF Link : [https://gre.kmf.com/practise/qr/234](https://gre.kmf.com/practise/qr/234)

> Write Power Prep 2 and register for exam if your score is around your desired score. 

## Third Month

**Focus** : Next few days - Revise everything where you're lacking & solve practice tests.

1. After completing everything, you can practice this Fill in the Blank **Verbal** questions of different difficulty levels from KMF.
    - 844 questions - 228 sections - At your own pace
    - KMF Link: [https://gre.kmf.com/practise/tc](https://gre.kmf.com/practise/tc)
2. Practice tests from ETS Official Guide
3. Solve Mixed Practice Sets from ETS Verbal & Quant Book
4. Keep Revising Vocab & Quant Formulas
5. Extra : Write Power Prep Plus if you feel like.
